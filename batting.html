Batting Statistics

Common hitting metrics and how to read them.

Key Formulas

  • AVG = Hits ÷ At-Bats
  • OBP = (Hits + Walks + HBP) ÷ (AB + BB + HBP + SF)
  • SLG = Total Bases ÷ At-Bats
